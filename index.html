BTC Signal Alert — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

BTC Signal Alert ("the Service") is a personal, automated notification tool that sends Bitcoin trend-signal alerts (a buy / sell / hold indicator based on public historical price data) to a single, pre-configured recipient by email, and optionally by SMS text message. This policy explains what information the Service uses and how it is handled.

Information We Collect

  • Email address — the address the alerts are emailed to.
  • Mobile phone number — if SMS delivery is enabled, the recipient number the alerts are sent to.

This information is provided directly by the operator of the Service during setup. The Service does not collect data from the public, does not use cookies, and does not track website visitors.

How We Use Your Information

The email address and phone number are used solely to deliver the Bitcoin signal alerts and a periodic status summary that the recipient has asked to receive. They are not used for any other purpose.

SMS / Text Messaging

  • Message frequency varies; expect a small number of messages per month (alerts are sent only when the signal changes, plus one daily summary).
  • Message and data rates may apply.
  • Reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe from text messages. Reply HELP for help.

Sharing of Information

No mobile information or phone numbers are shared with, sold to, or rented to any third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Contact information is not shared with any third party except the messaging and email service providers (e.g., the SMS provider and the email provider) strictly as needed to transmit the alerts you have requested. These providers are contractually prohibited from using the information for their own purposes.

Data Retention

The Service stores only the minimum configuration needed to operate and retains no message history beyond what the messaging/email providers retain for delivery.

Not Financial Advice

Alerts are generated from automated technical indicators based only on past prices. They are informational and are not financial advice and do not predict future results.
